Public IIoT Catalog

A list of industrial products that help automate integration.

Minimum Criteria for Product Submissions:

  1. Product must have literature accessible at submitted URL without registration that validates the submission.
  2. Product must support a well-known information scheme, as listed in the submission form. If your information scheme is not included in the selection list, select "other" and enter the missing scheme for consideration.
  3. Product must support a well-known communication protocol, as listed in the submission form. If your communicaiton protocol is not included in the selection list, select "other" and enter the missing protocol for consideration.
  4. Supporting OPC-UA Data Access alone is not sufficient for this catalog. Despite Data Access having wide adoption and providing a level of data readability, this benefit does not provide sufficient automation of integration.
  5. Supporting MQTT alone is not sufficient for this catalog. While MQTT automation integration by transporting data in a scalable way, this benefit is offset by the manual integration required to parse out custom information models when not using a well-known encoding and information scheme.
